My latest Unity cheap trick - annotating game objects by appending annotations to their names. This scheme does have the advantage of showing the annotation in the Hierarchy view. For example, the menu scene in HyperBowl has a Quit button not appropriate for the iOS version, so I marked it to-delete when I make the next branch copy for an iOS build.
