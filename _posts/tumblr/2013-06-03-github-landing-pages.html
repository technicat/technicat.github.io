--- layout: post title: GitHub Landing Pages date: '2013-06-03T16:37:04-07:00' tags: [] tumblr_url: https://fugugames.tumblr.com/post/110612480801/github-landing-pages --- A really cool feature of GitHub is the landing page that’s provided with each repo. I’m taking advantage of it for my Unity iOS book, since I have the book examples on GitHub, anyway. Among the conveniences, easy authoring (markdown format and an assortment of themes), and quick links to download a zip of the distribution or visit the repo. I’m having trouble settling on a theme, though, so I’ve been alternating.
