I only noticed today that my version of HyperBowl on dimeRocker Stack - the first incarnation of the dimeRocker Facebook platform, is no longer running, so I got to try out the Facebook remove-your-app functionality. I reviewed the announcements on the dimeRocker developer forums and saw that Stack was scheduled to shut down on March 1, replaced by the dimeRocker Arcade (HyperBowl is already on it) Here’s hoping the Arcade lives longer!
