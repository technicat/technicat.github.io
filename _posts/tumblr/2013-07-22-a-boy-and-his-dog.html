I’m a sucker for loyal dog stories. The one that gets me the most is that of Hachiko, with the Futurama version a close second. And then there’s the Don Johnson classic (I mean that sincerely, this is his best movie), A Boy and His Dog. I saw it first in college and again at Comic Con last week. It’s as good as I remember. This might pique your interest - it’s based on a Harlan Ellison story. Also - telepathic dog in a post-apocalyptic world.
