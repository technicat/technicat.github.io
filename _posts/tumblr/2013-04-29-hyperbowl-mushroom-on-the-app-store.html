My latest experiment with a new HyperBowl lane is based on the Mushroom Land environment from the Unity Asset Store. I purchased it during one of the Asset Store sales - anything that looks good and is $20 or less, why not? Right now it’s on the App Store as a free ad-supported lane and is somewhat bare bones. Before promoting it to the full HyperBowl app, it could use some ambient sound, maybe shorten the lane…actually, the lane comes in pieces that I jammed together, and they don’t even really match, I’m thinking it might be fun to have those pieces move up and down. Stay tuned.
