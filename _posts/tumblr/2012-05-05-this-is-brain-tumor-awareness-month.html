May is Brain Tumor Awareness Month, and I’m more keenly aware of this than I might otherwise be, as I just attended a very moving and inspirational Celebration of Life for a friend’s husband who passed away recently from brain cancer. His obituary just gives a hint of the amazing selfless spirit he exhibited during this ordeal. Another friend is participating in a Livestrong ride in his honor.
