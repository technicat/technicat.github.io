WordsEye allows you to Type a Picture by creating 3D graphics from text descriptions. It’s a new way to turn your imagination into images and to communicate online.

“Watch your language! Type a picture!”

Input text: The large black cat is on the ground. The ground is sand. The ground is shiny. The sky is ice. The brown box is behind the cat. A large Matisse lamp is 1 feet to the left of the cat. The large Matisse lamp is 2 feet in front of the box. A large yellow bird is 4 inches above the box.
