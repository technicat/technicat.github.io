It’s not clear to me that issuing press releases results in increased HyperBowl app sales, but there does seem to be a correlation between press releases and the number of Facebook fans. Looking at the Facebook Insights graph, the page starts out with just a handful of fans, then after a month it bumps up to fifty, probably when I decided to impose on a bunch of my Facebook fans (I refrained from blasting everyone on my friends list, but I sort of regret it now, since I can’t remember to whom I’ve sent fan invites). There was also a press release around that time, but I’m not sure if that coincided with the jump. However, the two recent ascents do seem to coincide with the most recent two press releases. I can only speculate that my attempt at a media blitz does bring in new players of the web player.
