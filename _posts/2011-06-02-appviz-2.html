I upgraded to AppViz 2 today, which addresses the two major issues I had with the original AppViz - tracking iAd sales and the lack of a cumulative sales graph that also showed the breakdown among multiple products. The way to show that is in the Dashboard, which now has a customizable time range like the Graph display (and both have convenient drop-down selectors for typical time periods, like last seven days, last 30 days, all, etc.)

The yellow is HyperBowl, the red is Fugu Maze and the orange denotes iAd revenue from Fugu Bowl, the top speckles at the end are from Fugu Maze on the Mac App Store, and the rest are the individual HyperBowl lanes that are now free. Ain’t it pretty?
