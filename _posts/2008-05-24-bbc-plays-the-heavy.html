I was perusing Neil Gaiman’s blog last week and saw mention of the BBC ordering the operator of the web site Mazzmatazz to take down postings of original Doctor Who knitting patterns she created depicting the characters Ood and Adipose (according to this article, the Doctor Who producers were more enthusiastic about the patterns). After some public outcry, the BBC has backed off a bit and decided to adopt the patterns as official BBC products.
