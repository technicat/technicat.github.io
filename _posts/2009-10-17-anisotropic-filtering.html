Darn, I realized after updating the HyperBowl Classic widget that my latest reimport of the assets clobbered the texture anisotropic filtering settings, which improves the appearance of textures viewed at sharp angles (instead of head-on). It makes a big difference - I fixed it in the web player, so you can see the difference between the two. Here’s the web player:

The rings look pretty smooth, eh? Now take a look at the the widget:

Notice there’s more blurring and blotchiness in the wood texture of the rings, and it’s even more distracting when you see it in motion, giving a rippling effect (oh, well, next update…) The iPhone apparently doesn’t support anisotropic filtering (or at least not much), so this artifact is also visible in the iPhone version.
