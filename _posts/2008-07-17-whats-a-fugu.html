When I listed Fugu Games on the Dreamhost Site of the Month forum (garnering an average of 2 points out of 10), one reviewer asked :“What’s a Fugu?”. It seems to me it would have been faster to just type “fugu” in the browser search box, but a fugu is the poisonous Japanese puffer fish served as a delicacy. Some of you may recall the Simpsons episode. See the wikipedia article for more information.

I came up with the name a few years ago after watching a segment on fugu on the Food Network. Tasty, with a hint of danger - perfect name for a game company!
