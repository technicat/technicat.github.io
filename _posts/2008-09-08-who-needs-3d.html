Travelling down memory lane, I found I’m able to play some old interactive fiction titles, a la Infocom, like this Scott Adams adventure (check out his lecture), by installing Zoom and downloading ZCode games from the Interactive Fiction Archive. I’m no better at text adventures than 3D games, but at least the resulting death is less gory.

One of these days, I might try out Inform, a natural language based system for designing interactive fiction. It looks pretty slick.
